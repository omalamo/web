Hexagon Travel

Taking a leaf out of the Sunday supplements

I created a web concept for a pitch. Hexagon are a high-end luxury travel company, with a focus on expert guides, local knowledge and authentic experiences. This brought the Sunday travel supplement to mind and a design reflecting elements of this style were used as a basis for the design: pairing italic subheadings with sans serifs, use of flat colour and line patterns all resonate with this print heritage.
